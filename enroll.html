Frequently asked questions

How long do I have access after enrolling?

Your portal access does not expire. Once enrolled and approved, you have unlimited access to your certification modules.

Can I work through modules on my phone?

Yes. The portal is fully responsive and designed for phone and tablet use with the same features as desktop — including read-aloud and interactive slides.

Do I need to complete modules in order?

Within a certification, modules unlock sequentially. Across certifications, you must complete each level before beginning the next.

What if I don't pass the exam?

Each certification includes 3 exam attempts at no additional charge. The exam is open-book — you may refer to all course materials during the exam.

Is group pricing available?

Yes. Contact SCWA for team pricing for organizations enrolling 5 or more participants.
