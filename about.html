About the St. Charles
Watersheds Alliance

A Midwest-based organization dedicated to advancing professional practice in green infrastructure and stormwater management through education, certification, and community.

Our mission

Building expertise in green infrastructure across the Midwest

The MidAmerica Watersheds Alliance was founded to address a critical gap in the professional development landscape: water resource practitioners at every career level needed rigorous, accessible credentials in green infrastructure design, management, and policy.

Our certification series — developed by leading landscape architects, civil engineers, ecologists, and municipal stormwater managers — provides the structured pathway that the GI profession has needed.

From entry-level practitioners completing Certification 1 to senior professionals earning the Master of Green Infrastructure designation, MAWA credentials represent a recognized standard of professional competency in the field.
