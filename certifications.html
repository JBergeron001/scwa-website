About MAWA

A professional development program advancing green infrastructure expertise across the American Midwest — serving engineers, landscape architects, planners, and municipal stormwater professionals.

Our Mission

Building GI Expertise Across the Midwest

The MidAmerica Watersheds Alliance was established to address a critical gap: water resource practitioners at every career level needed rigorous, accessible credentials in green infrastructure design, management, and policy.
Our certification series — developed by leading landscape architects, civil engineers, ecologists, and municipal stormwater managers — provides the structured professional pathway that the GI profession has needed.
MAWA is a professional development program of the St. Charles Watersheds Alliance, bringing regional credentialing to a broader Midwest audience.
