Platform features

Built for working professionals

Self-paced & on-demand

Start, pause, and resume any time. Every slide is individually navigable — no forced sequencing or expiration dates.

Read-aloud accessibility

Every slide read aloud with word-by-word highlighting and adjustable speed (0.5× to 2×) for every learner.

Multi-language voice

TTS in 7+ languages — English, Spanish, French, German, Mandarin, Arabic, and more. Saves to your profile.

Mobile & desktop

Full experience on any screen — study on the job site, in the office, or anywhere in between.

Interactive slides

Clickable hotspot diagrams, embedded reference links, and short video clips bring technical content to life.

Verified credentials

Pass the exam to earn your SCWA certificate — downloadable for your portfolio, LinkedIn, and CE records.

How it works

From enrollment to credential in four steps

Everything happens inside the SCWA learning portal — from creating your account to downloading your certificate.

Create your account & enroll

2

Study at your own pace
